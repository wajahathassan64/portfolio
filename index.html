Founder, designer, and sole engineer

  • Built end-to-end as a one-person team — design, iOS engineering, AI pipeline, App Store launch.

  • iCloud Sync via CloudKit with runtime activation and seamless multi-device sync.
  • Home Screen Widget (WidgetKit) and StoreKit 2 subscriptions ($9.99/mo · $69.99/yr) with restore-purchase and entitlement enforcement.
  • Localized onboarding, daily reminder notifications, CSV export, recurring expenses, zero-data-collection privacy posture.
